Expectations Agreement

Things we need to agree on

As a Pilot, you are representing Netlify in a semi-official capacity.
You should feel encouraged to be yourself! But at the same time, it's important we agree that, as long as you are a Pilot, you will:

  • Not use language or engage in actions that can be construed as racist, sexist, homo or transphobic, or otherwise discriminatory.Forums members should never feel unwelcome because of their background.
  • Not make any statements that are disparaging or damaging to Netlify or the Jamstack in general. Healthy debate and a diversity of opinions is encouraged, but please keep your wording neutral.
  • Not use your elevated status to insinuate or act as if you are a paid Netlify employee or otherwise achieve financial gain.
  • Not use your status as a Pilot to stalk, harass, or otherwise violate the personal boundaries of anyone in the forums.
  • To respect the authority of Netlify staff, who have the final say in running the forums and communicating with customers.


If someone's experience in the forums is negatively impacted by something you say or do, and they bring it to our attention, we'll chat with you about what went wrong.
If a Netlify staff member sees you saying or doing something inappropriate, we'll also chat with you about it and aim towards a resolution.
If it's a serious violation, we may revoke your Pilot status at any time. If your Netlify account has been upgraded, these upgrades will also be revoked without warning should a serious violation occur.
Even if you are a Pilot, the general Terms of Service and Code of Conduct do still apply.
